About Us

Wakulima Smart Farm was founded in 2020 with a vision to transform agriculture in Kenya through the use of modern technology and sustainable farming methods. We believe that every farmer deserves access to the knowledge, tools, and markets needed to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower smallholder farmers across Kenya by providing access to modern farming techniques, quality inputs, and reliable markets — improving livelihoods and ensuring food security for all.

Our Vision

To be the leading agricultural organization in East Africa, driving innovation and sustainability in farming for future generations.

Our Values

  • Innovation — Embracing modern technology in farming
  • Sustainability — Protecting the environment for future generations
  • Integrity — Being honest and transparent in all we do
  • Community — Supporting and uplifting local farming communities

Our Team

We are a team of passionate agriculturalists, agronomists, and technology experts working together to bring the best farming solutions to Kenyan farmers. Our team has over 20 years of combined experience in crop farming, livestock management, and agricultural training.
